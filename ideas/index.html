Ideas

Meant to serve as a general list of ideas that I haven't had the time or energy to invest in, but would like to document somewhere.

If you have a solution to one of these, or happen to have implemented one of them yourself, let me know!

Name Notes
Kitchen Management
November 24, 2020

A tool to manage a home kitchen. Ideally, it would track recent purchases and keep pantry and fridge ingredients up to date.

Additionally being able to calculate what ingredients in the kitchen are currently allocated toward a weekly menu would be nice.

Integrating with local grocery APIs would make this ideal, but the major stores have APIs that are locked down to partners.

Grocy is an open-source kind of this, but appears to be a little broader (home management). Maybe I can provide some UI work to the project, but it's not what I'm looking for.
Road Trip Sharing
July 15, 2016

Inspired by a cross-country roadtrip to Utah. Social roadtrips where you can share where you're going with other people making roadtrips. Maybe save photos in a way that lets you build automatic slideshows.

Obvious privacy concerns with tracking location data. Would need clear privacy controls.

Integration with Google and Facebook for easy location services and sharing seems like a must.
